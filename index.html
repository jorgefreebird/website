Planetary Comunity

Modern technologies give us a chance to move the world to a new level - to build one great human community. Watching what's happening around the world in real time ignites the synapses of our global consciousness.

The Internet is already the largest community in history. It crosses all borders and cultures, thanks to it there are no more faraway countries. The Internet has become a planetary infrastructure for communication and collaboration. Tools and knowledge of one nation now belong to all nations.

Digital activism and green thinking

Combining modern technology and creative ideas, passionate visionaries are finding more and more tools to speak about important social and environmental issues and creating avant-garde projects to give life to development initiatives are changing the world for the better.

Creative colaboration

Art history shows that artists have always been looking for new forms of art and unconventional ways to express their ideas and feelings. At the beginning of the 20th century, various avant-garde movements challenged the traditional perception of art, declaring that a true artist can create art from anything. This is how the mixed art was born. This trend continues today, and modern technologies play a huge role in its evolution.

Why should I choose Interactive Media and System?

This course is suitable for people who want better understanding of modern technologies and how to use them creatively in their work or art. Students can deepen the knowledge gained at their bachelor's degree while others can find new means of self-expression. If you are a tireless experimenter, a passionate dreamer or/and want to study technologies in the context of art you should choose Interactive Media and Systems.
