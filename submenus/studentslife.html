Our Acommodation on José Régio complex

The P. PORTO SAS currently have six student residences, four of which are located in the municipality of Porto (Campus 1) and two in Vila do Conde (Campus 2). The accommodation capacity of these structures is currently 317 students.

In each residence, each student finds their space of comfort and well-being.

In fulfilling its fundamental mission, it is about guaranteeing everyone access to the fundamental conditions for pursuing studies in accordance with all needs.
